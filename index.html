about

Born in California but raised with a nomadic lifestyle leaving nowhere to call home, Abby is a floater of sorts. Change is an old friend, and the need for adaptation the norm, the but one thing that has remained untouched is her passion for life and expression.

Abby currently attends Northeastern University, studying for her bachelor of fine arts in game design and animation, with minors in computer science, entrepreneurship, and theater.
